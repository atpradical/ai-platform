Returns are available within 14 days from the date of purchase, provided that the product has not been used and the original packaging is preserved. To arrange a return, please contact our support team through the website contact form.

Delivery across Russia takes from 3 to 7 business days, depending on the region. Delivery within Moscow is carried out the next day after placing the order.

Payment can be made by bank card, via the Fast Payment System (SBP), or in cash upon delivery. Installment payment is available for orders over 5,000 rubles.

All products come with a 12-month warranty from the date of purchase. A warranty claim must be confirmed with a receipt or an order number from your personal account.
